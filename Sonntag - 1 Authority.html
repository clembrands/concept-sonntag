About Dr. Sonntag

A surgeon who treats the person, not just the X-ray.

Dr. Jeffrey R. Sonntag is an orthopedic surgeon serving patients across Northern Indiana, with a practice built on clear communication, conservative-first thinking, and outcomes that hold up over a lifetime of activity.

"My job isn't to get you through surgery — it's to get you back to the things you measure your life by."

From weekend athletes and high-school competitors to grandparents determined to keep up, Dr. Sonntag tailors every plan to the life a patient is trying to protect — exhausting non-operative options first and reserving surgery for when it will genuinely deliver.
